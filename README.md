# 🛒 Black Friday Sales Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

This project performs Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on the Black Friday Sales dataset to uncover customer purchasing behavior and identify key business insights.

The goal is to understand how different customer demographics and product categories influence purchasing patterns during Black Friday sales events.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze customer purchasing behavior
* Identify trends across age groups and genders
* Explore the impact of occupation on purchases
* Study product category performance
* Generate actionable business insights through visualization

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

* Python 🐍
* Pandas
* NumPy
* Matplotlib
* Seaborn
* Jupyter Notebook

---

## 📂 Dataset Information

The dataset contains customer transaction information collected during Black Friday sales.

### Features Included:

* User ID
* Product ID
* Gender
* Age
* Occupation
* City Category
* Stay in Current City Years
* Marital Status
* Product Categories
* Purchase Amount

## 🔍 Key Insights

✅ Customers aged 26–35 showed strong purchasing activity.

✅ Purchase behavior varied across occupations.

✅ Product categories contributed differently to overall revenue.

✅ Customer demographics had a noticeable impact on spending patterns.

## 📌 Conclusion

The analysis reveals important customer purchasing patterns that can help businesses improve marketing strategies, target the right customer segments, and optimize product offerings during seasonal sales events.
